Newly Featured Artist: Yoshi

by eric

Ever since his rise to fame in the 2000s, Yoshi has since expanded his talents beyond exploring the world with Mario and friends. Who knew this little dinosaur would be such a great music artist?

His music has become a fan-favorite here on osu!, garnering over 1 million plays on maps with his music collectively.

We hope players and mappers alike love this new addition to the Feature Artists of osu!
