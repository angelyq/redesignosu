Newly Featured Artist: Isabelle

by max

After a year-long hiatus, Isabelle is back! What better way to return to the scene than to join our Featured Artists section? Let's hear from Isabelle herself what she has in store for us!

"I've spent a great deal of my time off thinking about how I would make my comeback. Inspired by the calming waters from Tortimer's Island and the trials and tribulations of being a mayor's assistant for a year or two, you should stay tuned for some amazing tunes in upcoming video games!" -Isabelle

And there you have it! We hope you're just as excited as we are to add Isabelle to our Featured Artist Section!
